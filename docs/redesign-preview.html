How it works

From your resume to your next job in three steps.

01

Upload, in any language

Drop your resume — PDF, Word, or even a photo. We support Spanish, French, Arabic, Chinese, Hindi, and English. We translate and structure it automatically.

02

See real matched jobs

We compare your skills to live US job listings using meaning — not just keywords — so a payment-processing engineer in Argentina gets matched to fintech roles, not generic "engineer" results.

03

Improve and apply

Your AI coach suggests specific edits to make your resume stronger for US employers — and builds you a properly-formatted US version, ready to download.

Real matching, not keyword soup

See exactly how well each job fits your background — and why.

Most job sites rank by how recently the listing was posted, or how loudly the employer paid to be featured. EmployAI ranks by how well your skills and experience actually match the role.

  • Color-coded match scores — see which jobs are worth your time at a glance.
  • Watch scores rise as you accept resume improvements.
  • Save and track applications through interview to offer.
